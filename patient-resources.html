Patient Resources

Patient Rights and Responsibilities

As a patient, you have the right to:

  • Receive care that is respectful and dignified.
  • Understand your treatment and options.
  • Access your medical records.

As a patient, you also have the responsibility to:

  • Provide accurate medical history and information.
  • Follow your treatment plan as discussed with your doctor.
  • Respect healthcare providers and other patients.

Medical Records and Billing

Resources for Patients and Families

We provide various support services to help you and your loved ones navigate healthcare challenges:

  • Support groups for chronic conditions and mental health.
  • Counseling services for patients and families.
  • Educational workshops on health and wellness.
