Patient Rights and Responsibilities

At FUTO Ambulance Services, we are committed to ensuring that all patients and families understand their rights and responsibilities when receiving care:

  • Right to respectful and compassionate care.
  • Right to privacy and confidentiality of medical records.
  • Responsibility to provide accurate medical history.
  • Responsibility to adhere to the agreed treatment plan.
